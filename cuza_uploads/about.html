About The Site


This site allows students to easily search clubs and events on campus that directly relate to their interests. Students can make changes to their interests, clubs they are interested in, events they would like to attend and search for clubs directly.

Clubs can use this site to provide details about their student organization including a bio of what they do and upcoming events. Clubs can also make it easy for student to search for them or their events by using tags for students to search.
